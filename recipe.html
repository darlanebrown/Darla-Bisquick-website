Butter Bisquick recipe

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup Butter softened
  • 3 cups Flour, sifted
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 1 cup Water

Baking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375*F.
  2. Stir fluor with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter, water and buttermilk.
  4. Add eggs one at a time.
  5. Mix with a wooden spoon.
  6. Gradually stir in the dry ingrediants slowly.
  7. Roll the dough out in flour do not mix in the extra flour.
  8. Take a bisquick cutter or hold a small glass upside down, make
    circles.
  9. Add them on the lightly grease cookie sheet.
  10. Bake them for 20 minutes or until done.
